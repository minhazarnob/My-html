Mymensingh, a city steeped in history and culture, lies in the heart of Bangladesh, cradled between the Brahmaputra and Old Brahmaputra rivers. This vibrant urban center serves as a melting pot of tradition and modernity, where age-old customs blend seamlessly with contemporary life. Renowned for its academic institutions, Mymensingh is a hub of intellectual activity, housing prestigious universities and research centers that contribute significantly to the nation's academic landscape.
